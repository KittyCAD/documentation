Zoo is built with security in mind from the ground up. We care deeply about
the security of our product and our customers' information.

If you have discovered a potential security vulnerability in Zoo, 
review our [security policy](https://github.com/KittyCAD/.github/blob/main/SECURITY.md) and afterwards report the bug. 

If your issue is not security-related, please reach out via our [community support forum](https://community.zoo.dev/).

### Contact information

Refer to our [security policy](https://github.com/KittyCAD/.github/blob/main/SECURITY.md) to learn how to get in contact with us.
