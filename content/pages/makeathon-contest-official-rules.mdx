# ZOO MAKEATHON OFFICIAL CONTEST RULES

**Last Updated**: April 15, 2026

**NO ENTRY FEE. NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED.**

The Zoo Makeathon (the “Contest”), organized and sponsored by Zoo Corporation (“Zoo”), is a
contest meant to highlight what happens when strong design instincts meet the powerful tooling
available in the Zoo Design Studio. The objective of the Contest is to create a CAD design using Zoo
Design Studio that inspires others to learn from, adapt, or expand upon your work. Your design can
serve any industry, solve any problem, or explore an innovative concept. Successful entries will
demonstrate creativity and technical skill that engage and inspire the broader design community.
Please see the Official Contest Rules below for additional information.

The Contest begins on April 28, 2026 at 12:01 AM PT and ends on May 5, 2026 at 11:59 PM PT (the
“Contest Period”).

and agree to be bound by these “Official Contest Rules.” IF YOU DO NOT ACCEPT THESE
OFFICIAL CONTEST RULES IN THEIR ENTIRETY, YOU MAY NOT PARTICIPATE IN THE
CONTEST OR SUBMIT AN ENTRY (AS DEFINED BELOW).

## 2. Eligibility. The Contest is a skill-based contest open to legal residents of the U.S. [and Canada]
[(excluding Puerto Rico and Quebec)], eighteen (18) or older, as of the date of entry, who have an
active e-mail account and Internet access throughout the Contest Period (each, an “Entrant”).
[Entrants under the age of 18 must have permission from their parents or legal guardians to
participate.] Employees of Zoo and its affiliates, subsidiaries, advertising, promotion, fulfillment or
other coordinating agencies, individuals providing services to Zoo through an outsourcer or temporary
employment agency during the Contest Period, and their respective immediate family members and

Entrants must have a Zoo account to submit an Entry. Entries must be created from scratch by the
Entrant in Zoo Design Studio using only tools available within the platform including, for example,
Sketch Mode, KCL and Zookeeper. Entrants will receive Zookeeper reasoning time credits in Zoo Design Studio for use
during the Contest Period. Upon conclusion of the Contest Period, each Entrants’ accounts will revert
to the subscription tier held by such Entrant prior to the Contest Period.

(c) Share your Entry on either YouTube, Discord, X, LinkedIn or Instagram using the

## 6. Judgement of Entries and Selection of the Winner. All qualified Entries will be judged by
Zoo’s [product development team], who are experts in the fields of [CAD samples]. Entries will be
judged based on the following criteria:

(a) 20% Execution: Is your Entry well designed and thoughtfully made?
(b) 20% Originality: Is your Entry fresh, surprising, or worth talking about?
(c) 20% Community Response: Does your Entry capture the attention of the CAD
community and earn engagement (e.g., “likes” and downloads) on Aquarium?
(d) 20% Purpose: Does your Entry solve a problem, serve a use case, or explore an idea in a
meaningful way?
(e) 20% Remixability: Will other people want to learn from your Entry, iterate on it, or
build on it?

The Entrant whose Entry earns the highest overall score will win. The decisions of the judges will be
final. If any ties must be broken to determine the winners, the judges will choose the winning Entry
based on their sole discretion.
