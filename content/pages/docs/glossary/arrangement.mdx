---
title: Arrangement
excerpt: A specific layout of an assembly to convey an idea or concept in CAD
synonyms:
    - layout
    - assembly concept
domains:
    - hardware-engineering
---

An arrangement refers to a layout with specific positioning and orientation of components in an assembly. An arrangement can also be used to convey an idea with several replacement components. For example, a car assembly can have different tires in each arrangement to show what the overall car assembly would look like with different tire sizes.
