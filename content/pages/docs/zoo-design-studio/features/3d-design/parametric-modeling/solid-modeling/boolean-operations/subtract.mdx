title: Subtract
excerpt: Remove overlapping solid volume with subtract operations in Zoo Design Studio.
sidebarPosition: 1
---

Use Subtract to carve one solid out of another, forming cavities, cutaways, and negative-space
details. It is the primary boolean for body-against-body material removal.
