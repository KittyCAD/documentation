---
title: Construction Geometry
excerpt: Control whether sketch geometry participates in profile regions in Zoo Design Studio.
sidebarPosition: 1
---

Construction geometry remains in the sketch as reference geometry, but it does not create closed
profile regions on its own.

Use construction geometry for layout, alignment, centerlines, symmetry axes, and dimension
references that should guide the sketch without becoming part of the modeled boundary.

Profile geometry, by contrast, participates in closed sketch regions and can define material that
will later be extruded, revolved, or otherwise used by downstream features.
