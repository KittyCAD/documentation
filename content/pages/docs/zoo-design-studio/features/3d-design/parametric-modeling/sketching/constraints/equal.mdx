---
title: Equal
excerpt: Constrain sketch entities to equal size in Zoo Design Studio.
sidebarPosition: 3
---

The Equal constraint keeps selected entities at the same size, such as matching line lengths or
circle diameters. Use it to preserve symmetry and repeated proportions without duplicating
dimensions.
