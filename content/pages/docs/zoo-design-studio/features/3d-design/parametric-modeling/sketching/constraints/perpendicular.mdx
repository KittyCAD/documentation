title: Perpendicular
excerpt: Constrain sketch segments to remain perpendicular in Zoo Design Studio.

The Perpendicular constraint forces two sketch segments to maintain a right-angle relationship. Use
it to preserve orthogonal geometry in rectangular and machine-style profiles.
