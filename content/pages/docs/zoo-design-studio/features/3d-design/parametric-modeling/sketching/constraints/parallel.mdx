---
title: Parallel
excerpt: Constrain sketch segments to remain parallel in Zoo Design Studio.
sidebarPosition: 1
---

The Parallel constraint keeps two line segments parallel as the sketch changes. Use it to preserve
constant direction across profile edges, guide rails, and layout geometry.
