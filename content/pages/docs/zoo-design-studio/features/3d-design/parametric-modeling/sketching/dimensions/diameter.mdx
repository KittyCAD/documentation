---
title: Diameter
excerpt: Control circular sketch geometry with a diameter dimension in Zoo Design Studio.
sidebarPosition: 3
---

The Diameter dimension defines the size of circular geometry by its full diameter. Use it for
circles and circular profiles when the design intent is based on hole size, boss size, or nominal
diameter.

Double-click an existing diameter dimension label to edit the diameter value after it has been placed.
