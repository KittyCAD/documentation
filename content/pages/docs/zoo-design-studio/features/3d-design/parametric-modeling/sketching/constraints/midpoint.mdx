title: Midpoint
excerpt: Constrain sketch geometry to the midpoint of another entity in Zoo Design Studio.
sidebarPosition: 8
---

The Midpoint constraint places a point or endpoint at the center of another sketch entity. Use it to
keep profiles centered on an edge without adding extra distance dimensions.
