title: Symmetry
excerpt: Constrain sketch geometry symmetrically around a reference in Zoo Design Studio.

---

The Symmetry constraint keeps selected sketch entities mirrored around a reference line. Use it when
matching geometry should remain balanced as dimensions or other constraints change.
