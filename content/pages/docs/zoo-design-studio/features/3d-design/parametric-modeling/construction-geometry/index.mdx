Construction geometry creates the references used to position and orient later features. These tools
are especially useful in multi-step models and assembly-aware workflows.

This section includes
[Offset Plane](/docs/zoo-design-studio/features/3d-design/parametric-modeling/construction-geometry/offset-plane)
and [Helix](/docs/zoo-design-studio/features/3d-design/parametric-modeling/construction-geometry/helix).
